We are developing a Unity Chess game.

Current AI Status
=================

The game supports two modes:
1. Multiplayer (Photon Fusion)
2. Vs AI

The AI only controls the BLACK pieces (team == 1).
The human player always controls WHITE.

The AI move is triggered ONLY ONCE after White finishes a move.

The trigger happens inside Chessboard.MoveTo() after:

isWhiteTurn = !isWhiteTurn;

If SelectedMode == VsAI and it becomes Black's turn, Chessboard starts a coroutine that waits 3 seconds and then calls

AIPlayer.Instance.TryMakeMove();

The AI is NOT called from Update().
This bug was already fixed because calling it from Update() caused the AI to move continuously.

----------------------------------------

Current AI Architecture

AIPlayer.cs contains:

- TryMakeMove()
- GetPieceValue()

AIMove contains:

- Piece
- Target
- Score

Checks.cs contains:

GetLegalMovesForPiece()

which returns ONLY legal moves that do not leave the king in check.

The AI already uses GetLegalMovesForPiece().

Therefore:

✓ AI never performs illegal moves.
✓ AI never intentionally leaves its own king in check.

----------------------------------------

Current AI Scoring

Each legal move gets a Score.

Current implemented scoring:

Capture Pawn
+100

Capture Knight
+300

Capture Bishop
+300

Capture Rook
+500

Capture Queen
+900

Capture King
+10000 (only theoretical)

Penalty:

If the same piece moved on the previous AI turn:

Score -= 60

----------------------------------------

Move Selection

All moves are sorted descending by Score.

The AI does NOT always pick the best move.

Instead it randomly selects among the best 2 moves.

This was done to avoid robotic behavior.

----------------------------------------

Current Limitations

The AI is intentionally EASY.

It still does NOT evaluate:

- center control
- king safety
- piece development
- forks
- pins
- skewers
- discovered attacks
- hanging pieces
- defended squares
- castling preference
- opening principles
- endgame strategy
- minimax
- alpha-beta pruning

It simply scores individual legal moves.

----------------------------------------

Difficulty Levels

Easy

Current heuristic AI.

Medium

Current heuristic AI with better scoring.

Hard

Minimax depth 2.

Expert

Minimax depth 4.

----------------------------------------

IMPORTANT DESIGN GOAL

Do NOT make the AI unbeatable.

This game is intended for casual players.

The AI should feel like a beginner/intermediate human.

It should occasionally miss strong moves.

It should not always find the strongest move.

Player enjoyment is preferred over chess engine strength.

----------------------------------------

Current Priority

STOP AI work unless a bug is found.

The AI is currently "good enough".

Focus should now return to Fusion multiplayer implementation.

AI improvements should only be resumed after multiplayer is fully complete.
